JP Script – SalesTool + Pitch Flow

Read straight down. This is the core “Hello → SalesTool → GREAT NEWS → 2 conditions → credit framing → close” flow.

1) Intro + Recording

“Hello, my name is David with JG Wentworth, this call is recorded for quality… Let’s see if we can get you some better savings, okay?”
  • Say intro exactly as written
  • Match calm, confident tone (not hypey)

2) Verify + Send SalesTool Link

Verify: • Address • Date of birth • Last 4 of SSN Then say: “I’m going to send you a secure link so we can look at the same numbers together on your screen.”
  • Verify address
  • Verify DOB
  • Verify last 4 of SSN
  • Place on brief hold and send SalesTool link

3) Results Screen → Email/Text Invite

• Pull up the Results screen and verify balances and accounts FIRST • Go to Calculator tab and click Email (send email + text SalesTool invitation)

4) Hello Screen + Turn Phone Sideways

“When you see the ‘Hello’ screen with your name, tap the green button. Turn your phone sideways so it fills the whole screen for you.” “On my side I’m only going to use two tabs with you: • Your Credit Report • Your Future Savings That way you’re only looking at what actually matters.”

5) GREAT NEWS Transition (No Loan → Restructuring)

“You didn’t qualify for any loan option, because of your credit utilization. But I have GREAT NEWS…” “What you WERE approved for is our interest-free debt restructuring program. What we’re able to do here at JG Wentworth is work directly with your creditors and wipe away all future compounding interest on these enrolled accounts. On top of that, we’re usually able to get a discount off the principal balances based on: • the high interest you’ve already paid • and the financial or medical hardships you’ve gone through. That way, instead of throwing almost 70% of your money at interest, you’re finally paying down the actual balances.”

6) Two Conditions

Condition 1 – No More Charging “With debt restructuring, because your creditors are agreeing to reduce balances based on that discount, you won’t be able to use these cards anymore after the first payment goes into the program.” Condition 2 – Credit Impact “Credit factor wise — don’t be surprised if there’s a little step back in your score in the early stages. It can take 3–4 months before we see the first resolutions go through. Any delinquencies that show up are temporary, and our clients typically see things recover in about 6 months once the resolutions start hitting and the balances are dropping.”

7) Late Pays / Confidentiality Framing

“That’s a great question. The option itself is confidential on your credit report — it’s designed that way to protect your future borrowing options. Our focus here is really on your debt-to-income ratio, not just the score. Once we get the debt under control, your overall financial life improves — and that’s when your score starts to actually mean something again.”

8) Check for Questions → Next Steps Bridge

“Any other questions before I go over the next steps with you?”
  • Pause and let them ask anything
  • Answer using your objection toolkit if needed

9) Expense Report → ACH → Docs

Flow for the close: 1. Expense Report • Confirm income, rent/mortgage, key bills, and realistic leftover 2. ACH Info • Grab bank name, routing, account, and confirm checking 3. Agreement / Docs • Send DocuSign while on the call • Walk them through term, deposit, fee, cancel-anytime • Confirm all signatures and “Finish” clicked

Post-Quote Checklist (1–6)

Use this AFTER they say the quote sounds good. Go straight down the list.

1) First Deposit / Pay Date

“Which paycheck feels safest for the first deposit?”
  • Confirm pay schedule (weekly / bi-weekly / etc.)
  • Choose paycheck that feels safest
  • Set first draft date 7–14 days out
  • Repeat date back to client

2) Banking Details

“Grab the checking account you use for bills so we can set up the dedicated account.”
  • Bank name
  • Routing number (repeat back)
  • Account number (repeat back)
  • Confirm checking (not savings)

3) State Recording

“Because you’re in [STATE], there’s a specific disclosure/recording I need to play so everything is 100% clear and compliant.”
  • Check state model (Standard / NSL / Arnold & Smith)
  • Play correct recording from system
  • For FL – read FL block before sending agreement

4) Send Agreement

“I’m going to send the agreement while we’re on the phone so we can go through it together.”
  • Confirm email address (spell it back)
  • Confirm cell (if texting link)
  • Send agreement
  • Have them refresh inbox / check spam

5) Sign Together

“Open that email now and I’ll walk you through the key parts so you’re not guessing.”
  • Confirm they opened correct document
  • Highlight deposit, term, fee, cancel-anytime
  • Have them complete all signature/initial boxes
  • Confirm they clicked Finish / Submit

6) Explain What Happens Next

“Let me walk you through exactly what happens next so there are no surprises.”
  • Explain welcome/onboarding call timing
  • Reconfirm first draft amount + date
  • Set expectations about possible creditor calls
  • Mention portal / statements (if applicable)
  • Promise follow-up call after first draft
